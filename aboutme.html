About Me

Hello! I'm Melvin V — a 3D & Full-Stack Developer driven by curiosity, creativity, and a passion for merging art with technology. With a Master’s degree in Computer Science (M.Sc.), I’ve developed a strong foundation in software development, interactive media, and artificial intelligence.

My journey in tech started with designing small web applications and exploring how visual elements can come alive through code. Over time, I grew fascinated by the blend of 3D graphics, animation, and interactive web experiences. Today, I specialize in crafting immersive digital spaces using Three.js, Blender, and modern web technologies.

On the development side, I’m skilled in building end-to-end solutions — from designing intuitive front-end interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to developing scalable back-end systems using Python (Flask) and Java (Spring Boot). I love working on projects that challenge both my creative and technical thinking.

My interests go beyond traditional software — I’ve explored projects in voice-based authentication systems, deepfake detection, and AI-generated content analysis, reflecting my enthusiasm for emerging fields in deep learning and digital forensics.

Every project I build tells a story. Whether it’s a 3D interactive landing page, a responsive portfolio, or a machine learning application, I aim to create something meaningful — experiences that connect technology and human emotion.

I believe in continuous learning and love experimenting with new frameworks, animation techniques, and visual storytelling tools. My ultimate goal is to evolve as a developer who can bridge creativity and logic — transforming abstract ideas into immersive digital realities.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring visual design, experimenting with sound, and learning about advancements in artificial intelligence. I’m always open to collaborations that push the boundaries of what’s possible on the web.
